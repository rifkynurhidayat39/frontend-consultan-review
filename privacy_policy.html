Your Privacy Matters

Consultation Denpasar Institute ("Denpasar Institute", "we", "our" or "us") is deeply committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in connection with your use of our websites, mobile applications, and online or offline services (collectively referred to as our "Services"). By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Our goal is to ensure that you understand clearly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. We adhere to all applicable privacy and data protection laws, and we apply best practices to safeguard your information responsibly.

Collecting and Using Personal and Anonymous Information

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Services to you, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address, date of birth, and other identifying information you provide when registering or using our services.
  • Educational Information: Academic history, achievements, certificates, and other educational documents relevant to your program of interest.
  • Usage Information: Data about how you interact with our platform, including activity logs, learning preferences, and usage patterns.
  • Technical Information: Device data, IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, and other technical information that helps us optimize your experience.

Data Storage and Security

Your data security is our top priority. We implement comprehensive security measures, including:

  • Data Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols to ensure maximum security.
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to your information, and this access is strictly monitored.
  • Regular Backups: Your data is routinely backed up in secure locations to prevent data loss.
  • 24/7 Monitoring: Our security systems are continuously monitored to detect and prevent security threats.

Information Usage and Sharing

We use your information responsibly and transparently. Here's how we use and share information:

  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage data to enhance the quality and effectiveness of our educational programs.
  • Communication: Sending important updates, new program information, and relevant promotional materials (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Institutional Cooperation: Sharing information with partner educational institutions when necessary for your application or enrollment process.
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with legal obligations and responding to legitimate requests from authorities.
